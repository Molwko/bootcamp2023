Kamohelo Moleko

Soweto based individual Kamohelo Moleko is the co-founder of FONT - Functional Orchestrated Needle Test.
Born in Tladi and raised by a single mother, he had aspirations of making life better in the townships he was raised in.
Inspired by his parents love for clothing, he then set his sights on starting a local/luxury clothing brand alongside childhood friend Kagiso Mamakoko, who is the immediate co-founder and partner of the clothing brand.
